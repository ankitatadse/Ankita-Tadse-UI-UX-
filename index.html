Common questions

FAQ

What type of roles are you looking for?+
I'm looking for full-time UI/UX Designer or Data Visualization Designer roles — ideally at product companies or agencies building enterprise tools, SaaS products, or data-driven applications. I'm especially interested in roles at the intersection of UI design and dashboard/data storytelling, having worked across healthcare, finance, insurance, and automotive domains.
What's your design process like?+
I start with research — user interviews, competitive analysis, and workflow mapping. Then I move to IA and wireframes before any hi-fi work. I believe clarity before creativity leads to better outcomes and fewer redesigns.
What tools do you use?+
My primary design tool is Figma — from wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes and design systems. For data visualization and dashboard design, I work with Power BI and Tableau. I also use Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, and AI tools like Figma AI, Claude, and ChatGPT to accelerate ideation and iteration.
Are you available for freelance projects?+
Yes — I take on select freelance projects alongside my job search. I prefer focused engagements: mobile apps, web products, or redesigns with a clear scope and a real user problem at the center.
How can I see more of your work?+
All my public work is on this portfolio. I also have several case studies in progress (DMS, Retailer's App, Clock-In) which will be posted here shortly. Feel free to reach out directly and I can share work-in-progress files.
